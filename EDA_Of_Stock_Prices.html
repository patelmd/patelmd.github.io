Exploring historical stock data of top bank institutes during the financial crisis until 2016


An exploratory data analysis of bank stocks and see how they progressed throughout the financial crisis until 2016. This project is not meant to be use as a financial analysis or financial advice. Rather, It is an exercise in understanding data analysis in finance domain. This dataset has been collected from Google Finance.
